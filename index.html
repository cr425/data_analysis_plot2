Lending Club

This plot illustrates the different type of home ownerships and the loan amounts that are borrowing. This plot tells us a few things:

  • Individuals who rent, own, or have mortgages take out the majority of loans.
  • The loans for renters, owners, and mortgage holders all range to $35,000.

Upon further exploration, one can determine the unique characteristics behind the most popular ownership class.
